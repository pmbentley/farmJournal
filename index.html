Little Running Farm Journal

Eventually, all things merge into one, and a river runs through it.
- Norman Maclean


When Rob and I moved to the family farm in March, 2020 I continued the practice, initiated by Rob's parents who had the farm before us, of chronicling events in a print journal.


This is a digital version of one year in the life of the Little Running Farm - where our family's life, children, dogs, and a river, run.
